Syujet / Synopsis

The Story

SCENE 01
Discovering the Stones

In Old Tashkent's winding streets, four teens stumble upon luminous stones that pulse with a heartbeat from the cosmos.

SCENE 02
Decoding the Signals

Shahina patches her terminal into the stones. Patterns emerge — coordinates, frequencies, a desperate warning from light-years away.

SCENE 03
Cosmic Danger

A dark void entity targets Tashkent as its entry point. With the city asleep, the four teenagers are all that stand in its way.

SCENE 04
Saving Tashkent

United, they harness the stones' starlight energy in one last stand — and the city glows with a dawn it almost never saw.
