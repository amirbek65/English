SCENE 01 Discovering the Stones

EXT. OLD TASHKENT ALLEYWAY — DUSK. Narrow streets glow with a faint bioluminescent haze. The city hums with electric life. AMIRBEK, SHAHINA, KRISTINA, and AKBARHON stumble upon a collapsed section of pavement. Below the rubble, four stones pulse with unearthly blue-violet light.

AMIRBEK MY LINE
Wait — everyone stop. Do you hear that? It's like… a voice, but not from anyone here. It's coming from the ground.
SHAHINA
A voice? Amirbek, you're hearing things. It's probably a broken gas pipe or some underground cable.
KRISTINA
No, wait — she's wrong. I hear it too. Something is whispering. And those stones… they're glowing.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
We pick them up. All four of us, at the same time. On three. One… two… three!
AKBARHON
Bold plan, fearless leader! What could possibly go wrong? Other than, you know, an alien takeover?
AMIRBEK MY LINE
Akbarhon — serious, please! (lifts his stone) It's warm. Like it's been waiting for us. This is real. This is really happening.
SCENE 02 Decoding the Star Messages

INT. SHAHINA'S BEDROOM — NIGHT. Holographic code streams across every surface. The four stones orbit above a makeshift receiver. The whispers grow louder — layered, melodic, urgent. SHAHINA types frantically. The others lean in.

SHAHINA
I've isolated the frequency. It's not random noise — these are structured pulses. Binary, but with a third state. Whoever sent this is not from Earth.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
Then what are they saying? Shahina, translate it. We need to know if we're in danger.
KRISTINA
Look at the map — the signal is pointing directly at Tashkent. We're the target.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
Target for what? (stares at the pulsing stone in his hand) Are they warning us, or marking us?
AKBARHON
My stone just got hotter. And it showed me something — a dark mass, spiraling toward the solar system. That's not a metaphor, by the way.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
Then we have no time to waste. Shahina, keep decoding. Kristina, find every exit from the city center. Akbarhon — try not to blow anything up. (beat) Yet.
SCENE 03 Cosmic Danger

EXT. CHORSU ROOFTOP — MIDNIGHT. The sky above Tashkent has split open with a rift of absolute darkness. A void entity — vast, hungry, ancient — descends toward the city. Citizens below are frozen in confused panic. The four teens stand on the rooftop, stones blazing.

KRISTINA
It's here. It's actually here. The rift is 200 metres wide and growing. Amirbek — what do we do?!
AMIRBEK MY LINE
We stand our ground. If we run, everyone in this city disappears. I am NOT letting that happen!
SHAHINA
The stones resonate at the same frequency as the entity's core signature. If we synchronise all four — we might be able to push it back.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
(raising his stone toward the void) Then we synchronise. All of us. Together. NOW!
AKBARHON
For the record, I always knew my life would end on a rooftop. I just assumed it involved a kite.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
Focus! I can feel the stone pulling at something inside me — like it's reading my courage. Give it everything you've got. Every last bit.
SCENE 04 Saving Tashkent

EXT. CHORSU ROOFTOP — DAWN. The four stones pulse as one. A beam of pure starlight tears through the void entity, which screams — a sound like a dying galaxy — and begins to dissolve. The rift seals. The city exhales. Silence. Then, the first light of dawn floods Tashkent in gold and teal.

AMIRBEK MY LINE
(breathing hard, watching the rift close) It's working. Don't let go — whatever you do, don't let go!
KRISTINA
The rift is collapsing! Thirty percent… fifty… it's sealing!
SHAHINA
Signal confirmed — the entity is retreating. The stars… they're answering back. We did it.
AMIRBEK MY LINE
(the light fades from the stones; he looks at the sky, which is now painted in dawn colours) We whispered back to the stars… and they listened.
AKBARHON
So… same time next week? (everyone laughs, exhausted and relieved)
AMIRBEK MY LINE
(smiles, arm around the group) Tashkent is still standing. And so are we. That's enough. That's everything.
