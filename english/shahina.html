SCENE 01 Discovering the Stones

EXT. OLD TASHKENT ALLEYWAY — DUSK. The city buzzes with neon-lit evening traffic. Beneath the crumbled pavement, four stones pulse with impossible blue light. The team gathers, cautious and awestruck.

AMIRBEK
Wait — everyone stop. Do you hear that? It's like… a voice, but not from anyone here. It's coming from the ground.
SHAHINA MY LINE
A voice? Amirbek, you're hearing things. It's probably a broken gas pipe or some underground cable.
KRISTINA
No, wait — she's wrong. I hear it too. Something is whispering. And those stones… they're glowing.
SHAHINA MY LINE
(kneels, pulls out a sensor device) Thermal reading is off the charts. These aren't minerals from Earth. The crystal lattice is… entirely unknown to science.
AKBARHON
Great! Unknown alien rocks. I'll just add that to the list of things that are definitely not dangerous.
SHAHINA MY LINE
Don't be dramatic. Until we have data, we make no assumptions. (picks up her stone carefully) But I'm running a full spectral analysis the moment we get back.
SCENE 02 Decoding the Star Messages

INT. SHAHINA'S BEDROOM — NIGHT. Every screen blazes with alien code. Holographic rings float from the stones as Shahina's fingers blur across her keyboard. The whispers have become a coherent signal.

SHAHINA MY LINE
I've isolated the frequency. It's not random noise — these are structured pulses. Binary, but with a third state. Whoever sent this is not from Earth.
AMIRBEK
Then what are they saying? Shahina, translate it. We need to know if we're in danger.
SHAHINA MY LINE
(squinting at the screen) I'm building the translation matrix now. The message repeats — it's a warning. And… coordinates. These coordinates are ours. They know exactly where we are.
KRISTINA
Look at the map — the signal is pointing directly at Tashkent. We're the target.
SHAHINA MY LINE
Not just a target — a convergence point. Something is using our electromagnetic field as a doorway. And the window opens in… (goes pale) …fourteen hours.
AKBARHON
Fourteen hours. Cool, cool. Plenty of time for panic.
SCENE 03 Cosmic Danger

EXT. CHORSU ROOFTOP — MIDNIGHT. A tear in space opens above the city. The void entity's mass distorts the air around it. Electromagnetic chaos crackles. Shahina's device screams warnings. They have only minutes.

KRISTINA
It's here. It's actually here. The rift is 200 metres wide and growing. Amirbek — what do we do?!
SHAHINA MY LINE
The stones resonate at the same frequency as the entity's core signature. If we synchronise all four — we might be able to push it back.
AMIRBEK
Then we synchronise. All of us. Together. NOW!
SHAHINA MY LINE
(types last command into her device, then clutches her stone) Resonance protocol initiated. I've linked all four stones through the device. The moment we align our focus — it fires.
AKBARHON
What if it doesn't work? What if this is just how it ends?
SHAHINA MY LINE
It will work. The mathematics are sound. I trust the data. (looks up at the void) Now I need to trust us.
SCENE 04 Saving Tashkent

EXT. CHORSU ROOFTOP — DAWN. Starlight floods through the closing rift. The entity dissolves. Shahina watches her screen as every reading normalises. Silence replaces the chaos. Slowly, Tashkent stirs back to life below.

AMIRBEK
It's working. Don't let go — whatever you do, don't let go!
KRISTINA
The rift is collapsing! Thirty percent… fifty… it's sealing!
SHAHINA MY LINE
Signal confirmed — the entity is retreating. The stars… they're answering back. We did it.
AKBARHON
So… same time next week?
SHAHINA MY LINE
(reads the final readout, then closes her laptop softly) The stones are dormant now. The message from the stars… it says "well done." (pause) I didn't expect that part.
AMIRBEK
Tashkent is still standing. And so are we. That's enough. That's everything.
